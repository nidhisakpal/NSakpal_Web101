Hiking and Camping Trips

Hey! Do you love to hike and camp? I'm Nidhi Sakpal and I plan and host local hiking + camping trips in the NJ/NY area. I'm passionate about conservation and connecting with nature. These trips are intended to build a community of like-minded people who appreciate the outdoors.

What to Remember/ Bring Trips & Camps

The goal is to have fun, respectful, and always kind.

  • Arrive on Time After you've sent in your RSVP, you're commited. Make sure to honor your commitment and respect other people's time.
  • Take Care of Yourself Make sure to bring any necessary medication, the right shoes + clothing, and anything you anticipate needing to make sure you have a safe and enjoyable experience.
  • Be a Good Teammate Be friendly and inclusive — especially to people who may be new or quiet. Help clean up, carry things, and support others when needed. Respect everyone's space and belongings. Listen and follow instructions — they're there for everyone's safety.
